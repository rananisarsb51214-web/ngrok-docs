# ERR_NGROK_1120

### Message
Cannot use '-hostname=`HOSTNAME` with '-subdomain=`SUBDOMAIN` parameter.
You may either specify '-hostname=`HOSTNAME` or '-subdomain=`SUBDOMAIN` but not both.
